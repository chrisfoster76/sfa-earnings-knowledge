## Key Concepts (Quick Glossary)

- **ILR** — Individualised Learner Record. The data return providers submit to ESFA/DfE to evidence funded activity.
- **SLD** — Submit Learner Data. The portal through which ILR files are submitted.
- **Apprenticeship Levy** — 0.5% payroll tax on employers with pay bill > £3m. Funds sit in DAS (Digital Apprenticeship Service) accounts.
- **DAS** — Digital Apprenticeship Service. The employer-facing platform for managing apprenticeship funding.
- **EPAO** — End-Point Assessment Organisation. Independent body conducting final assessment of apprentices.
- **EPA** — End-Point Assessment.
- **OTJ** — Off-the-job training. Required element of all apprenticeship programmes.
- **ESFA** — Education and Skills Funding Agency (now part of DfE).
- **DfE** — Department for Education. Overall policy owner.
- **Academic Year** — Runs 1 August to 31 July.
